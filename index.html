LCS Class 28

We are Year Up New York / New Jersey Class 28. Our Learning Community is LC:S, named after our wonderful program manager Serena Tillman. We are instructed by the great Deodat Sharma and Kara Badette, who teach us all the skills we will need to succeed in our internships and future careers. LCS Class 28 is the first ever Year Up class to go virtual due to the COVID-19 outbreak, but the level of adaptability in overcoming these new and foreign obstacles from both staff and students is what makes Class 28 "The Best of the Best".
